# Meet Sofi — Your Learning Companion

Sofi is an AI tutor that lives in Slack. She helps you study anything — language learning, technical concepts, onboarding material, whatever you're working on — using spaced repetition, which means she quizzes you at exactly the right intervals so things actually stick.

---

## Getting Started

Just **DM Sofi in Slack** — find her in your direct messages the same way you'd message a colleague.

The first time you message her, she'll have a short conversation to learn how you like to be taught — teaching style, what motivates you, how you handle mistakes. Takes about 2 minutes. If you want to skip it and jump straight in, just say:

> *skip setup*

---

## What Sofi Can Do

### Study sessions
> *quiz me*

Sofi picks the questions you're most due to review and runs a session. She grades your answers, gives feedback in your preferred style, and updates your progress automatically.

> *quiz me on [topic]*

Study a specific topic. For example: *quiz me on Portuguese verbs* or *quiz me on Kubernetes*.

### Upload study material
Upload any **PDF or text file** to the chat and Sofi will process it into flashcards automatically. She reads the document, extracts key questions and answers, and adds them to your personal study deck.

### Check your progress
> *show progress*

See your overall mastery across all topics, how many questions you've reviewed, and your average score.

> *what needs work?*

Shows your weakest areas — the topics and question types with the lowest mastery scores.

### During a session
| Say this | What happens |
|---|---|
| Type your answer | Sofi grades it and gives feedback |
| `skip` | Skip this question |
| `end` | End the session early |
| `customize` | Change Sofi's teaching style mid-session |

### Update your preferences
> *customize*

Runs the setup questions again so you can change your teaching style, feedback preferences, or background knowledge.

## How Sofi Teaches

When you set up your profile, you choose:

**Teaching style** — Sofi can show up as:
- A wise mentor (thoughtful, Socratic)
- A martial arts master (direct, no-nonsense)
- A patient teacher (gentle, encouraging)
- A research advisor (rigorous, peer-level)

**What motivates you** — curiosity, achievement, play, or social contribution. Sofi frames her feedback differently depending on what drives you.

**How you handle mistakes** — whether you want direct correction or gentle encouragement.

She remembers all of this and adapts every response accordingly.

---

## Tips

- **Upload first, then quiz.** Give Sofi a document before your first session so she has material to work with.
- **Short sessions are fine.** Even 5–10 minutes of review makes a difference with spaced repetition.
- **Just talk to her.** If you're confused about something or want to ask a question, just ask — she's not only for quizzes.
- **She remembers you.** Your progress, profile, and study history are saved between sessions.
