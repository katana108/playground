# How to Upload Documents for Study with Sofi

When you want Sofi to explain, summarize, or create study materials from a document, you can share content in several ways.

## How to Share a Document

**Paste text directly:** Copy the text from your document and paste it into the chat. Works for any plain text — notes, articles, book excerpts, lecture slides, or study guides.

**Upload a file:** Attach a PDF, Word document (.docx), or plain text file (.txt) using the paperclip icon in the chat input.

**Share in chunks:** For very long documents, paste section by section and let Sofi know you're continuing the same document.

## What Happens Next

Once you share your material, Sofi will:
1. Identify the topic and your learning level
2. Write organized notes summarizing key concepts
3. Generate 15 Anki-style study questions across four thinking levels: **Recall, Explain, Apply, and Connect**

## Tips

- **Richer source material → richer study cards.** A detailed article produces deeper questions than a brief outline.
- **Tell Sofi your level** (beginner, intermediate, advanced) to adjust difficulty.
- **Tell Sofi your goal** — exam prep, deep understanding, quick review — and she will tailor the materials accordingly.
